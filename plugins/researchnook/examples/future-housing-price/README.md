# Future housing price example

This example shows the intended shape of a Researchnook seed for a
housing-price research report:

1. define the research brief with OSTIN;
2. choose a framework mix, usually PESTLE plus scenario forecasting;
3. scope geography, time horizon, and buyer audience;
4. plan sources and collect user-provided or accessible evidence;
5. assess evidence confidence;
6. analyze drivers and uncertainty;
7. produce base / upside / downside scenarios;
8. synthesize a Chinese decision brief and full report;
9. review claims, evidence, and caveats before publishing.

The seed deliberately does not include real market data or paid-source
access. Replace placeholders with user-approved sources before relying
on the report.
